Ning-Hsu (Albert) Wang

I am a Computer Vision research intern at Mediatek, Taiwan. I received my Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from National Tsing Hua Unviersity (NTHU) advised by Prof. Min Sun. During my graduate research, I worked with Prof. Wei-chen Chiu and Dr. Yi-Hsuan Tsai on 360° Stereo Depth Project. Afterward, I worked with Prof. Hwann-Tzong Chen on Planar Reconstruction. My research interest lies in Deep Learning and its applications, especially focusing on 360° VR/AR application and Robotics Perceptions.
